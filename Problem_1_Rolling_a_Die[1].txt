Problem 1: Rolling a Die

You roll a fair six-sided die.

1. What is the sample space of this experiment?
2. What is the probability of rolling a 4?
3. What is the probability of rolling an even number?

Problem 2: Flipping a Coin

You flip a fair coin three times.

1. What is the sample space of this experiment?
2. What is the probability of getting exactly two heads?
3. What is the probability of getting at least one tail?

Problem 3: Drawing a Card

You draw a card from a standard deck of 52 cards.

1. What is the sample space of this experiment?
2. What is the probability of drawing a heart?
3. What is the probability of drawing a face card (Jack, Queen, or King)?

Problem 4: Selecting a Marble

A bag contains 3 red, 2 blue, and 5 green marbles. You draw one marble at random.

1. What is the sample space of this experiment?
2. What is the probability of drawing a blue marble?
3. What is the probability of drawing a marble that is not green?

Problem 5: Tossing Two Dice

You toss two fair six-sided dice.

1. What is the sample space of this experiment?
2. What is the probability of getting a sum of 7?
3. What is the probability of getting doubles (both dice show the same number)?
